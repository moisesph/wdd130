History

We started as a small outfit with a big idea: share river adventures with families and friends. Over the years we've grown while keeping guide-led safety and great customer service as priorities.

Our routes vary by season — from gentle floats to expert rapids. We offer half-day trips, full-day runs, and special multi-day expeditions for experienced paddlers.
