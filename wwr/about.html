About Us - Dragoncito Expeditions

Our purpose is to make everything fun to you in the safest way and greatest! to make an activity who everybody can join and have a really good time.

History

I started with my boat on these waters with my dog. I fished from morning until midnight. One day a friend asked me to give him a ride on my boat; I agreed, and then he brought more people.

I noticed that people really like to go on challenging rafting trips, so this is how the job started. Now I am pleased that everybody enjoys this activity as I do, thanks to the help of our employees who make it possible.
